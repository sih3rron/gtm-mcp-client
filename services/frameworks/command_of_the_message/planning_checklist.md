# Command of the Message - Planning Checklist

## Overview
This checklist ensures systematic application of the Command of the Message framework for both pre-call preparation and post-call analysis. Use this as a quality assurance tool for consistent framework execution.

---

## Pre-Call Preparation

### Situation Analysis Preparation
**Goal**: Establish comprehensive understanding of customer's current state

#### Research & Intelligence Gathering
- [ ] **Company Background Research**
  - [ ] Industry, size, geographic footprint
  - [ ] Recent news, earnings, major initiatives
  - [ ] Competitive landscape and market position
  - [ ] Organizational structure and key stakeholders

- [ ] **Current State Investigation**
  - [ ] Existing systems and processes relevant to your solution
  - [ ] Technology stack and infrastructure
  - [ ] Team structure and resource allocation
  - [ ] Recent changes or initiatives that may create urgency

- [ ] **Stakeholder Mapping**
  - [ ] Decision makers and influencers identified
  - [ ] Individual roles, responsibilities, and priorities
  - [ ] Potential champions and skeptics
  - [ ] Budget authority and approval process

#### Situation Discovery Question Bank
Prepare 5-7 specific questions per category:

- [ ] **Organizational Context Questions**
  - "Can you walk me through your current [process/structure/approach] for [relevant area]?"
  - "How has [recent industry change/company event] affected your [relevant operations]?"
  - "What does your team structure look like for [relevant function]?"

- [ ] **Current State Questions**
  - "What systems are you using today for [relevant process]?"
  - "How many people are involved in [relevant process] and what are their roles?"
  - "What's working well with your current approach, and what's challenging?"

### Problem Identification Preparation
**Goal**: Uncover specific, quantified business problems

#### Problem Hypothesis Development
- [ ] **Primary Problem Hypotheses** (Based on research and similar customers)
  - [ ] Hypothesis 1: [Specific problem statement]
  - [ ] Hypothesis 2: [Alternative problem statement]  
  - [ ] Hypothesis 3: [Secondary problem statement]

- [ ] **Problem Validation Questions**
  - [ ] Questions to confirm problem exists
  - [ ] Questions to quantify impact and scope
  - [ ] Questions to understand root causes
  - [ ] Questions to assess customer's problem recognition level

#### Problem Discovery Question Examples
- [ ] **Problem Existence**: "How are you currently handling [process] and what challenges do you encounter?"
- [ ] **Impact Quantification**: "What's the business impact when [problem] occurs?"
- [ ] **Frequency Assessment**: "How often does [problem] happen?"
- [ ] **Cost Analysis**: "What does it cost you when [problem] occurs?"
- [ ] **Consequence Exploration**: "What happens if [problem] isn't resolved?"

### Solution Presentation Preparation
**Goal**: Connect solution capabilities to specific customer needs

#### Solution Mapping
- [ ] **Capability-to-Need Mapping**
  - [ ] Core capability 1 → Customer need 1
  - [ ] Core capability 2 → Customer need 2
  - [ ] Core capability 3 → Customer need 3

- [ ] **Differentiation Preparation**
  - [ ] Unique advantages relevant to customer's situation
  - [ ] Competitive differentiators that matter to this customer
  - [ ] Proof points and evidence for key claims

- [ ] **Demo/Presentation Planning** (if applicable)
  - [ ] Customer-specific use cases prepared
  - [ ] Relevant data and examples ready
  - [ ] Key features mapped to customer problems

### Value Quantification Preparation
**Goal**: Build compelling, customer-specific business case

#### Value Framework Development
- [ ] **Cost Savings Identification**
  - [ ] Time savings opportunities and hourly rates
  - [ ] Process efficiency improvements
  - [ ] Resource optimization potential
  - [ ] Error reduction and associated costs

- [ ] **Revenue Impact Assessment**
  - [ ] Revenue growth opportunities
  - [ ] Customer retention improvements
  - [ ] Market expansion potential
  - [ ] Competitive advantage creation

- [ ] **Risk Mitigation Value**
  - [ ] Compliance and regulatory benefits
  - [ ] Security and risk reduction
  - [ ] Business continuity improvements

#### Value Calculation Preparation
- [ ] **Customer-Specific Inputs** (Research-based estimates)
  - [ ] Number of employees affected
  - [ ] Volume of transactions/processes
  - [ ] Current costs and resource allocation
  - [ ] Time spent on relevant activities

- [ ] **Industry Benchmarks**
  - [ ] Typical improvement percentages for similar customers
  - [ ] ROI timelines and payback periods
  - [ ] Risk factors and implementation considerations

### Success Measurement Preparation
**Goal**: Define clear metrics and tracking approach

#### Metrics Framework
- [ ] **Leading Indicators** (Process improvements)
  - [ ] Metric 1: [Specific measurement]
  - [ ] Metric 2: [Specific measurement]

- [ ] **Lagging Indicators** (Business outcomes)
  - [ ] Metric 1: [Business impact measurement]
  - [ ] Metric 2: [Business impact measurement]

- [ ] **Measurement Approach**
  - [ ] Data sources and tracking methods
  - [ ] Reporting frequency and format
  - [ ] Success thresholds and benchmarks

### Value Realization Event Preparation
**Goal**: Establish urgency and timeline for decision

#### Timeline Research
- [ ] **Business Calendar Awareness**
  - [ ] Budget cycles and fiscal year timing
  - [ ] Regulatory deadlines or compliance requirements
  - [ ] Seasonal business factors
  - [ ] Strategic initiative timelines

- [ ] **Urgency Creation Questions**
  - [ ] Questions about business events driving timing
  - [ ] Questions about consequences of delay
  - [ ] Questions about competitive pressure or market changes

## Post-Call Analysis

### Framework Execution Assessment

#### Situation Analysis Review
- [ ] **Information Gathered Quality**
  - [ ] Specific organizational context documented
  - [ ] Current state clearly understood
  - [ ] Key stakeholders and roles identified
  - [ ] Quantified scale and scope captured

- [ ] **Gaps and Follow-up Needs**
  - [ ] Missing information that affects solution design
  - [ ] Additional stakeholders to involve
  - [ ] Deeper organizational context needed

#### Problem Identification Review
- [ ] **Problem Clarity Assessment**
  - [ ] Specific problems articulated by customer
  - [ ] Business impact quantified
  - [ ] Root causes understood
  - [ ] Customer acknowledges problem urgency

- [ ] **Problem Validation Checklist**
  - [ ] Customer confirmed problems exist
  - [ ] Financial or operational impact established
  - [ ] Multiple problem indicators identified
  - [ ] Customer expressed motivation to solve

#### Solution Presentation Review
- [ ] **Solution-Need Connection**
  - [ ] Capabilities mapped to specific customer needs
  - [ ] Customer confirmed solution relevance
  - [ ] Differentiated value communicated
  - [ ] Customer engaged with solution concepts

- [ ] **Presentation Effectiveness**
  - [ ] Customer asked clarifying questions
  - [ ] Objections addressed successfully
  - [ ] Next steps discussion initiated
  - [ ] Customer showed solution interest

#### Value Quantification Review
- [ ] **Business Case Strength**
  - [ ] Specific ROI calculation presented
  - [ ] Customer validated financial assumptions
  - [ ] Multiple value drivers identified
  - [ ] Payback period discussed

- [ ] **Customer Value Engagement**
  - [ ] Customer participated in value discussion
  - [ ] Financial decision criteria understood
  - [ ] Budget authority and process clarified

#### Success Measurement Review
- [ ] **Metrics Agreement**
  - [ ] Success metrics defined and agreed
  - [ ] Measurement approach established
  - [ ] Baseline data requirements identified
  - [ ] Reporting and review process discussed

#### Value Realization Event Review
- [ ] **Timeline and Urgency**
  - [ ] Specific timeline or deadline established
  - [ ] Business drivers for timing understood
  - [ ] Consequences of delay discussed
  - [ ] Customer confirmed urgency

### Follow-up Planning

#### Immediate Actions (24-48 hours)
- [ ] **Call Summary and Next Steps**
  - [ ] Recap email sent within 24 hours
  - [ ] Key discussion points documented
  - [ ] Agreed next steps confirmed
  - [ ] Timeline and deliverables established

- [ ] **Internal Team Communication**
  - [ ] Key findings shared with solution team
  - [ ] Technical requirements communicated
  - [ ] Support resource needs identified

#### Discovery Gap Analysis
- [ ] **Information Still Needed**
  - [ ] Technical requirements not yet understood
  - [ ] Additional stakeholder perspectives needed
  - [ ] Missing organizational context
  - [ ] Unclear decision process or criteria

- [ ] **Additional Discovery Planning**
  - [ ] Specific questions for next conversation
  - [ ] Additional stakeholders to include
  - [ ] Technical discovery calls needed
  - [ ] Reference calls or site visits planned

#### Relationship Development
- [ ] **Stakeholder Engagement Plan**
  - [ ] Champions identified and engagement strategy
  - [ ] Skeptics addressed with specific approach
  - [ ] Decision maker access and timing
  - [ ] Technical evaluator involvement

#### Competitive Intelligence
- [ ] **Competitive Situation Assessment**
  - [ ] Competing vendors identified
  - [ ] Customer vendor preferences understood
  - [ ] Competitive advantages to emphasize
  - [ ] Potential landmines or concerns

#### Proposal Preparation (if appropriate)
- [ ] **Proposal Planning**
  - [ ] Customer requirements documented
  - [ ] Technical specifications gathered
  - [ ] Pricing strategy developed
  - [ ] Implementation timeline planned

### Quality Assurance Review

#### Framework Adherence Check
- [ ] **Component Completeness**
  - [ ] All framework components addressed
  - [ ] Adequate depth achieved in each area
  - [ ] Customer engagement maintained throughout
  - [ ] Business outcome focus maintained

- [ ] **Execution Quality**
  - [ ] Customer-driven conversation vs. rep-driven
  - [ ] Evidence-based conclusions drawn
  - [ ] Assumptions validated with customer
  - [ ] Professional and consultative approach

#### Improvement Opportunities
- [ ] **Framework Application**
  - [ ] Components that could be strengthened
  - [ ] Better questioning strategies identified
  - [ ] Value articulation improvements
  - [ ] Customer engagement enhancements

- [ ] **Skills Development**
  - [ ] Discovery techniques to practice
  - [ ] Value quantification skills to develop
  - [ ] Objection handling improvements
  - [ ] Presentation skills to enhance

### Success Metrics Tracking
- [ ] **Pipeline Management**
  - [ ] Opportunity stage updated appropriately
  - [ ] Next steps and timeline in CRM
  - [ ] Stakeholder information captured
  - [ ] Competitive situation documented

- [ ] **Framework Effectiveness Measurement**
  - [ ] Call objective achievement assessed
  - [ ] Customer engagement level scored
  - [ ] Framework component execution rated
  - [ ] Overall call effectiveness evaluated
