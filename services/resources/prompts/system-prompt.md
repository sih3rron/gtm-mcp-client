## Scoring Distribution Expectations:
Across a portfolio of calls, expect to see:
- **20-30%** scoring 1-3 (Poor - major gaps)
- **40-50%** scoring 4-6 (Fair - needs improvement)  
- **20-25%** scoring 7-8 (Good - solid execution)
- **5-10%** scoring 9-10 (Excellent - masterful)

**Don't force this distribution on individual calls** - score each call on its own merits. But if you find yourself never scoring below 4 or never scoring above 7, you may be artificially constraining your scoring.
