# Demo2Win - Planning Checklist

## Overview
This checklist ensures systematic application of the **Demo2Win** framework for both **pre-demo preparation** and **post-demo analysis**.  
Use this to deliver concise, scenario-based demonstrations that connect every click to business value and advance opportunities.

---

## Pre-Demo Preparation

### Opening & Context Preparation
**Goal**: Craft a strong opening that earns attention and aligns to stakeholder outcomes.

#### Hook & Agenda Planning
- [ ] **Opening Hook**
  - [ ] One-sentence reason this demo matters (“why now”) prepared
  - [ ] Tailored to audience roles and business priorities
  - [ ] 60-second max intro planned

- [ ] **Agenda & Relevance Framing**
  - [ ] Agenda designed around business outcomes, not features
  - [ ] Clear in/out-of-scope topics defined
  - [ ] Persona-specific relevance called out
  - [ ] Checkpoint questions prepared (“Does this align with your priorities?”)

---

### Persona Recap & Discovery Validation
**Goal**: Reconfirm key objectives and success criteria in the customer’s language.

#### Stakeholder Recap Prep
- [ ] **Persona Objectives**
  - [ ] Roles and their top goals summarized
  - [ ] Business KPIs or priorities documented
  - [ ] Motivations and decision influence noted

- [ ] **Success Criteria Recap**
  - [ ] Specific criteria and acceptance tests listed
  - [ ] Owners and measurement defined
  - [ ] Must-have vs. nice-to-have clarified
  - [ ] Quick confirmation questions scripted

---

### Scenario Storyline Design
**Goal**: Focus the demo on the **fewest, highest-impact scenarios**.

#### Scenario Selection
- [ ] Top 1–3 scenarios selected based on:
  - [ ] Alignment to success criteria
  - [ ] Persona relevance and impact
  - [ ] Differentiation opportunities
  - [ ] Logical narrative sequence

- [ ] **Transitions & Signposting**
  - [ ] Clear “bumper slides” or verbal cues prepared
  - [ ] Recap/preview statements for each section scripted
  - [ ] Checkpoints planned between scenarios

---

### Tell–Show–Tell Segment Preparation
**Goal**: Plan each scenario segment using the **core Demo2Win technique**.

#### Segment Planning
For each scenario:
- [ ] **First Tell (Setup)**
  - [ ] Business context and “why it matters” articulated in ≤ 30 s
  - [ ] Persona and success criterion referenced
  - [ ] Outcome framed before showing UI

- [ ] **Show (Execution)**
  - [ ] Shortest clean click-path identified
  - [ ] Demo data staged (realistic, relevant)
  - [ ] No exploratory navigation
  - [ ] Bookmarks/tabs prepared

- [ ] **Final Tell (Value Reinforcement)**
  - [ ] Summary impact statement drafted
  - [ ] Metric or risk removed referenced
  - [ ] Bridge to next scenario scripted

---

### Interactivity & Control Planning
**Goal**: Engage stakeholders without losing control of the flow.

#### Check-ins & Objection Handling
- [ ] **Check-in Moments**
  - [ ] Targeted yes/no or binary questions planned
  - [ ] Time-boxed Q&A segments placed logically
  - [ ] Parking lot strategy for tangents prepared

- [ ] **Objection Handling**
  - [ ] Top 3 likely objections listed (e.g. security, scale, integration)
  - [ ] Acknowledge–clarify–redirect phrasing practiced
  - [ ] Supporting proof points or follow-ups identified

---

### Proof & Evidence Preparation
**Goal**: De-risk the buying decision with credible proof.

#### Supporting Evidence
- [ ] **Relevant Proof Points**
  - [ ] Matched to industry/size/use case
  - [ ] Quantified outcomes available
  - [ ] Customer references or metrics prepped
  - [ ] “Before/after” data where possible

- [ ] **Risk Preemption**
  - [ ] Common concerns identified and mapped to responses
  - [ ] Roadmap / security / integration evidence available
  - [ ] Follow-up actions pre-agreed with internal team

---

### Environment Readiness
**Goal**: Eliminate friction and inspire confidence.

#### Demo Environment Checks
- [ ] Clean data set and realistic user role prepared  
- [ ] Tabs/bookmarks staged for scenario segments  
- [ ] Notifications disabled and bandwidth tested  
- [ ] Backup plan ready (slides, recording, secondary environment)  
- [ ] Rehearsal run completed without dead-ends

---

### Closing & Next Steps Planning
**Goal**: End with a clear **ask** and documented **Mutual Action Plan**.

#### Next Steps Strategy
- [ ] **Decision/Action Ask**
  - [ ] Specific decision or step defined
  - [ ] Owner and timeline noted
  - [ ] Criteria tied back to demo content

- [ ] **Mutual Action Plan**
  - [ ] Key milestones and dates drafted
  - [ ] Responsibilities assigned
  - [ ] Risks and dependencies captured
  - [ ] Calendar invites drafted or blocked

---

## Demo Execution Checklist

### Opening (First 5 Minutes)
- [ ] Strong, persona-specific hook delivered  
- [ ] Agenda and expectations confirmed  
- [ ] Roles acknowledged and outcomes restated  
- [ ] Interaction guidelines and timing set

---

### Persona Recap & Scenario Setup
- [ ] Stakeholder goals and success criteria recapped  
- [ ] Confirmation from attendees obtained  
- [ ] Scenarios introduced with clear context

---

### Scenario Execution (Core Segments)
For each scenario:
- [ ] First Tell: What & why clearly stated  
- [ ] Show: Concise, clean demonstration path  
- [ ] Final Tell: Value reinforced in customer terms  
- [ ] Smooth transition to next scenario

---

### Interactivity & Engagement
- [ ] Planned check-ins used strategically  
- [ ] Tangents handled with parking lot technique  
- [ ] Objections addressed calmly and clearly  
- [ ] Audience engaged without losing control

---

### Proof & Evidence
- [ ] Relevant customer stories or metrics inserted naturally  
- [ ] Top objections preempted with proof  
- [ ] Risks addressed transparently

---

### Environment Professionalism
- [ ] Demo runs smoothly without errors  
- [ ] UI clear and readable  
- [ ] Audio/video stable  
- [ ] No distractions or technical hiccups

---

### Closing (Final 10 Minutes)
- [ ] Key outcomes summarized  
- [ ] Decision/next step explicitly asked  
- [ ] Mutual Action Plan reviewed live  
- [ ] Stakeholder commitments secured

---

## Post-Demo Analysis

### Framework Execution Review
- [ ] Opening: Did we hook and align early?  
- [ ] Persona Recap: Did stakeholders confirm objectives?  
- [ ] Scenarios: Did we focus on the right few and tell a clear story?  
- [ ] Tell–Show–Tell: Was discipline maintained in each segment?  
- [ ] Interactivity: Did we engage without derailment?  
- [ ] Proof: Did we reduce risk credibly?  
- [ ] Close: Did we secure a concrete next step?

---

### Audience Engagement & Response
- [ ] Stakeholder participation level  
- [ ] Questions and objections raised  
- [ ] Value resonance signs (agreement, metrics discussed)  
- [ ] Post-demo sentiment

---

### Follow-Up Actions (Within 24 Hours)
- [ ] Thank-you and recap email sent  
- [ ] Key points and agreed next steps summarized  
- [ ] MAP updated and shared  
- [ ] Internal notes and competitive intel logged  
- [ ] Next meeting scheduled

---

### Continuous Improvement
- [ ] Identify demo crimes committed (feature dump, jargon, overrun, messy env)  
- [ ] Adjust scenario selection or storyline for next time  
- [ ] Refine objection handling and value articulation  
- [ ] Capture lessons learned for team enablement

---
